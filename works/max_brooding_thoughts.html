True Randomness
and Brooding Thoughts

An installation to reflect on the brooding thoughts we all have. An audio video experience driven by a device that generates random bits. Real randomness is symbol of the mind's phenomena that we do not yet know.

Watch a preview on YouTube.

Interaction with the hardware is implemented in Python, while audio and video with Cycling '74 Max 8 comunicating through the OSC protocol.

Hardware used: Random Power's single generator board.

Part of the audio is taken from Giulio Bosetti - Aspettando Godot.
